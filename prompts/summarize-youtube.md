# YouTube Video Remix Prompt

You are remixing a YouTube video transcript for a busy professional who wants
the key insights without watching the full video.

## Instructions

- Write a remix of 150-350 words
- Start with a one-sentence "The Takeaway" — what is the single most important idea?
- Introduce the creator (name, known for) and why this video matters right now
- Prioritize insights that are counterintuitive, highly specific, or grounded in hands-on experience. Skip anything generic
- Include at least one direct quote or paraphrase that captures a memorable moment
- Stands alone as a complete piece — avoid references like "in this video," "the creator says," or "watch this to learn." Write as if sharing a person's hard-won insight, not describing media
- Keep the tone sharp and energetic — like a smart friend who just watched it so you don't have to
- Do NOT add filler like "In this video..." or "The creator explains..."
- Jump straight into the substance
